NameTypeDescription
◈ Iron Reach Mineral Fields Asteroid Belt Dense mineral fields that supply the Crimson Pact's industrial chain. Carbon and tungsten convoys run on strict schedules between here and the forges.
⬡ Iron Reach Mining Colony Station A no-frills mining station bolted to the largest asteroid in the mineral fields. Ore convoys launch on a schedule measured in ticks, not hours. The mess hall serves protein paste and carbon dust in roughly equal proportions, and nobody complains — everyone here is working toward the same forge.
● Ore Depot Planet Military supply world. Raw ore is weighed, graded, and loaded onto convoys bound for Blood Forge. Nothing leaves without documentation — Crimson bookkeeping is as precise as their aim.
● Rustfield Planet A barren world stripped of surface minerals generations ago. Deep-bore extraction shafts still operate beneath the crust, feeding the forges at Blood Forge.
☀ The Ember Sun A dim orange star the mining crews call The Ember — not much to look at, but it keeps the lights on. The war machine doesn't need a pretty sky. It needs ore.
