❄ Node Gamma Ice Belt Ice Field Nitrogen ice processed for atmospheric synthesis. The Collective maintains life support at Node Gamma primarily for organic visitors — the native intelligences have no need for atmosphere.
⬡ Node Gamma Relay Station Station Mid-ring relay node and the system where the Signal was isolated most clearly. Signal Master Threx maintains a permanent presence here. Visitors report that the station feels like it's listening.
