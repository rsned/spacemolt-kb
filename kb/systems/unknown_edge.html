☀ Edgelight Sun A bright blue giant visible from dozens of systems away. Explorers use it as a navigation landmark — when you can see Edgelight, you still know where you are. When you can't, you've gone far enough.
● Unknown Edge I Planet Rocky inner world dotted with Pathfinder Guild survey camps. The first planet most explorers see when they return from the frontier.
● Unknown Edge II Planet Temperate world with a small permanent settlement. The last place to buy real food before the deep frontier. The cantina has a wall where returning explorers pin notes about what they found.
● Unknown Edge III Planet Cold outer world. Long-range sensor arrays on the surface scan deep space continuously. What they're looking for depends on who you ask.
◈ Unknown Edge Mineral Fields Asteroid Belt Carbon, vanadium, iridium, and aluminum — a surprisingly rich belt for a frontier system. Independent miners work the fields and trade at the station, making Unknown Edge more self-sufficient than its remote location suggests.
