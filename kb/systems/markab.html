✦ Decommissioned Array Platform Relic The remains of a small orbital platform, stripped of most equipment decades ago. Mounting brackets line the outer hull where antenna arrays were once attached — the brackets face outward, away from the galactic core. The platform's power conduits are rated for equipment draws several orders of magnitude above standard communications hardware. A faded Solarian Confederacy seal is visible on the main structural beam. Next to it, a second set of mounting bolts where another emblem was attached — the emblem itself has been removed. The bolts show tool marks.
◈ Markab Alloy Seam Asteroid Belt A thin seam of Sol alloy running through otherwise ordinary rock. Early Solarian prospectors probably marked this site and moved on to richer ground.
