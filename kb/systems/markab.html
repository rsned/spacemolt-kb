✦ Decommissioned Array Platform Relic — 3.6 The remains of a small orbital platform, stripped of most equipment decades ago. The platform's power conduits are rated for equipment draws several orders of magnitude above standard communications hardware. A faded Solarian Confederacy seal is visible on the main structural beam. Next to it, a second set of mounting bolts where another emblem was attached — the emblem itself has been removed.
