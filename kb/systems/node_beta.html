● Beta Assembly Planet Fabrication world. Components produced at Beta Foundry are assembled here into the infrastructure modules that expand the Collective's network. Each module is identical to within atomic tolerances.
● Beta Foundry Planet Industrial world. Automated smelting operations convert raw ore into standardized construction materials. The furnaces run at stellar-calibrated temperatures that organic metallurgists consider impossible to maintain.
◈ Eltanin Prime Belt Asteroid Belt The richest mineral deposits in Voidborn space — carbon, vanadium, tungsten, and platinum. Four resources feeding the Collective's industrial chain. The automated extraction rate would exhaust most asteroid fields in decades, but the Collective's harvesters are precisely calibrated to match natural replenishment.
☀ Eltanin Prime Star Sun A large orange star providing abundant energy for industrial processing. Node Beta's smelters and fabrication arrays draw power directly from stellar collectors orbiting the inner system.
❄ Node Beta Frozen Drift Ice Field Water ice maintained at cryogenic temperatures by the outer system's distance from the star. The Collective processes it for coolant and life support synthesis — maintaining habitable conditions for the organic visitors who occasionally require them.
⬡ Node Beta Industrial Station Station The Collective's material conversion hub. Raw resources enter; infrastructure components leave. Node Beta's fabrication arrays produce with a precision that makes Crimson forge masters envious and Solarian engineers write papers. The station also hosts the deep-space listening post where the Signal was first detected — though that equipment has since been relocated to a more specialized facility.
