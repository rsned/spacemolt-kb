Points of Interest (6)
NameTypeDescription
● Frostfeld I Planet An ice world locked in permanent glaciation. Wind-carved methane spires rise kilometers above the frozen surface. The Pact assessed it for colonization and decided the cold wasn't worth fighting.
● Frostfeld II Planet Frozen outer world. Surface temperature cold enough to crystallize atmospheric gases. Crimson scouts mapped it once and never returned.
◈ Frostfeld Ice Flats Asteroid Belt Vast fields of nitrogen and water ice drifting in the dim starlight. Occasionally harvested by Crimson supply ships running low on coolant, but not worth a permanent operation.
◈ Frostfeld Thermal Vent Asteroid Belt Superheated plasma residue venting from deep volcanic fissures. Less productive than the weapons-testing grounds of Krynn, but still worth harvesting.
◈ Smelt Reef — Frostfeld Asteroid Belt Darksteel ore in quantities too small to support a full forge operation, but enough to be worth a detour for any weaponsmith.
☀ The Pale Sun A dim white dwarf bleeding the last of its heat into the void. Locals call it The Pale — everything here is cold, and the star does nothing to argue the point.
