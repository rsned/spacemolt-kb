NameTypeDescription
☀ Eventide Sun A warm yellow star that earned its name from returning explorers — the light here always feels like sunset, like the end of something. Heading out, Eventide is the last warm glow before the void. Coming home, it's the first.
● Last Light I Planet Small rocky world close to the star. Solar collection arrays power the system's infrastructure from here.
● Last Light II Planet Temperate world with scattered frontier settlements. The largest has a population of about two thousand — practically a city by Rim standards.
● Last Light III Planet Agricultural world. Hardy crops adapted to the local conditions feed the system's population and provision outbound expeditions.
● Last Light IV Planet Cold outer world. Long-range communication relays on the surface maintain contact with Unknown Edge and the deep frontier.
◈ Last Light Mineral Fields Asteroid Belt Carbon, tungsten, thorium, and aluminum — a diverse mineral base that makes Last Light the most self-sufficient outer ring system in the Rim. Thorium deposits in particular draw prospectors from across the frontier.
☁ Last Light Nebula Gas Cloud Dense gas pocket rich in hydrogen, argon, and plasma. The nebula's glow is visible from neighboring systems — part of how the system earned its name. The warm light against the void.
