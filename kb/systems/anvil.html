NameTypeDescription
● Anvil I Planet Weapons testing range. The surface is a cratered wasteland of impact sites, burned-out targets, and unexploded ordnance. Crimson R&D evaluates every new weapon design here before it enters production.
● Anvil II Planet Hardened underground bunkers store the Pact's strategic munitions reserve. Automated defenses engage anything that approaches without clearance.
◈ Anvil Proving Grounds Asteroid Belt Carbon and vanadium deposits mixed with weapons testing debris. New recruits occasionally mine here, dodging fragments from the latest round of ordnance trials in the adjacent testing lanes.
☀ Steelpoint Sun A steady white dwarf the weapons engineers call Steelpoint — chosen for its stability. The Pact needed a system where precision manufacturing tolerances wouldn't drift with stellar activity.
⬡ The Anvil Arsenal Station Final assembly for the Crimson Pact's weapons systems. Alloys from Blood Forge arrive as ingots and leave as turrets, railgun barrels, and armor plate. The station's forgemaster maintains a tradition: every weapon carries the serial number of the pilot who supplied its raw materials. A warrior's honor, stamped in steel.
