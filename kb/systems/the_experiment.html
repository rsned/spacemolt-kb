☀ Theta Serpentis Sun — 0.0 A dim red dwarf at the outer edge of Voidborn space, cataloged but unremarkable. The Collective chose this location for its isolation — whatever they're testing here, they don't want it near the core network.
● Experiment Prime Planet — 1.5 A world that shouldn't be habitable but is — atmospheric composition maintained by equipment that predates the Collective's arrival. Someone or something was here before them. The Collective hasn't shared what they found.
⬡ The Experiment Research Station Station — 2.0 The Collective's most remote and most classified facility. Positioned three jumps from Nexus Prime in a system with a pre-existing anomaly the Voidborn have never explained, the Research Station processes data that arrives encrypted and leaves the same way. Organic visitors are permitted but monitored. The station's purpose is officially 'fundamental research.' Nobody believes that's the whole story.
✦ The Array Relic — 3.2 A structure of unknown origin and purpose extending three kilometers in diameter. The Collective has surrounded it with processing equipment and analysis instruments. The Array predates human presence in this region of space. It is active. The Collective will not discuss what it does.
☁ The Experiment Haze Gas Cloud — 3.9 Thin hydrogen cloud providing supplementary fuel for the research facility. The cloud's unusual spectral signature suggests it may have been artificially seeded — though by whom, and with what, is another question the Collective declines to answer.
◈ Experiment Substrate Fields Asteroid Belt — 4.0 Carbon, palladium, and lithium deposits. The lithium is particularly significant — the Collective consumes it in quantities that don't match any known manufacturing process. What they're building with it is classified even within the Collective.
