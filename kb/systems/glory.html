● Crucible Prime Planet Garrison world. Military academies, live-fire training ranges, and shipyard facilities cover the temperate zones. The polar regions house the Pact's strategic reserve fleet in hardened underground hangars.
☁ Crucible Vents Gas Cloud Superheated argon and plasma vent from deep volcanic fissures on Crucible Prime's largest moon. The Pact harvests them for weapons manufacturing and shield testing.
☀ Fury Minor Sun A fierce red dwarf that shares its hue with Krynn's Fury. The garrison crews named it in tribute — where the capital burns, the Crucible smolders.
⬡ The Crucible Garrison Station Where warriors are forged. Every Crimson captain passes through the Crucible — weapons certification, ship qualification, and the combat trials that separate officers from the rest. The station doubles as a chokepoint checkpoint with its sister station at The Rampart. Nothing enters Krynn space without passing both.
