● Assembly World Planet Manufacturing complexes cover the northern hemisphere. Raw materials arrive from Gold Run and Treasure Cache; finished trade goods leave for Market Prime. The southern hemisphere is still undeveloped — room to grow.
☁ Factory Belt Haze Gas Cloud Industrial haze mixed with harvestable hydrogen and argon. The gas cloud's proximity to the manufacturing stations makes it a convenient fuel source for the factories' power systems.
⬡ Factory Belt Manufacturing Hub Station Where the Federation turns raw materials into trade goods. The Manufacturing Hub runs automated production lines around the clock, converting ore and gas into components the galaxy needs. Observant traders notice that shipments sometimes arrive from Treasure Cache without appearing on any Federation manifest — a clerical oversight, surely.
☀ Steadyburn Sun A reliable yellow star the factory managers call Steadyburn — consistent output means consistent production schedules, and the Federation appreciates predictability above all else.
