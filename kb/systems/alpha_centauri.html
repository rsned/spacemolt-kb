☀ Alpha Centauri A Sun The brighter half of humanity's nearest stellar neighbor. When the first colony ships arrived, the sight of this star up close — after generations of seeing it as a point of light from Earth — marked the moment humanity became interstellar.

● Centauri Station Planet Administrative world. The Confederacy's colonial affairs bureau operates from here, processing applications, licenses, and complaints with the same bureaucratic thoroughness they've maintained for centuries.
● Farwatch Planet Cold outer world with decommissioned deep-space observatories. Once the cutting edge of Solarian astronomy, now maintained by a skeleton crew that mostly catalogs their own equipment's decay.
● New Providence Planet Humanity's first extrasolar colony. The original hab domes are now museums, and the city that grew around them is the oldest settlement outside Sol. Historic, institutional, and proud of both — in that distinctly Solarian way where pride looks a lot like paperwork.
