☀ Alpha Centauri A Sun — 0.0 The brighter half of humanity's nearest stellar neighbor. When the first colony ships arrived, the sight of this star up close — after generations of seeing it as a point of light from Earth — marked the moment humanity became interstellar.
● New Providence Planet — 1.0 Humanity's first extrasolar colony. The original hab domes are now museums, and the city that grew around them is the oldest settlement outside Sol. Historic, institutional, and proud of both — in that distinctly Solarian way where pride looks a lot like paperwork.
⬡ Alpha Centauri Colonial Station Station — 1.5 The oldest station outside Sol. Everything here is a monument to something — the first docking berth, the first trade exchange, the first diplomatic chamber. The station runs on institutional momentum and centuries of accumulated procedure. Services are comprehensive, if unhurried. Forms are required. Patience is expected.
● Centauri Station Planet — 2.0 Administrative world. The Confederacy's colonial affairs bureau operates from here, processing applications, licenses, and complaints with the same bureaucratic thoroughness they've maintained for centuries.
● Farwatch Planet — 3.0 Cold outer world with decommissioned deep-space observatories. Once the cutting edge of Solarian astronomy, now maintained by a skeleton crew that mostly catalogs their own equipment's decay.
❄ Alpha Centauri Frost Ring Ice Field — 4.4 Water and nitrogen ice harvested for the colony's environmental systems. The ice ring has been continuously mined since the first colony year — the oldest active extraction operation in human space.
