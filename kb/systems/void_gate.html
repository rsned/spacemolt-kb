☀ The Red Lantern Sun An old red giant marking the edge of Outer Rim territory. Explorers call it The Red Lantern — the last familiar light before the void. Its ruddy glow has welcomed back more lost ships than any beacon.
● Void Gate I Planet Barren rock with emergency supply caches buried beneath the surface. Placed there by the Pathfinder Guild for expeditions that come back with empty holds and damaged ships.
