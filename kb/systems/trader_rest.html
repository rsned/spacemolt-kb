● Garden World Planet Agricultural luxury. Orchards, vineyards, and livestock ranches produce the finest food in Federation space. Nothing here is mass-produced — every item carries a provenance certificate and a price to match.
☀ Golden Hour Sun A gentle golden star that bathes the system in warm light. The Federation's wealthiest merchants named it Golden Hour — the light here always looks like money.
● Merchant's Landing Planet Where the Federation's trading families live. Mansions and estates stretch across manicured continents. The architecture is ostentatious by design — in the Federation, wealth displayed is wealth respected.
● The Promenade Planet A world devoted entirely to leisure. Resort complexes, entertainment districts, and private estates cover every temperate zone. More credits are spent here per capita than anywhere outside Haven itself.
⬡ Trader's Rest Resort Station Station Where the money goes to relax. The docking fees alone would fund a frontier outpost for a year, but the cantinas serve real food, the repair bays use genuine parts instead of salvage, and the neural arcades run Voidborn entertainment matrices. The Federation's merchant class lives here and commutes to Haven — because even traders need somewhere that isn't a trading floor.
