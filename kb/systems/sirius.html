☀ Sirius A Sun — 0.0 The brightest star in Earth's night sky, and humanity's second colony system. The Solarian Confederacy chose it for their primary research campus — prestige location for prestige science.
● Research Campus Planet — 1.0 The Confederacy's scientific crown jewel. University complexes, research laboratories, and lecture halls spread across the temperate zones. More doctoral theses are defended here per year than anywhere else in human space.
⬡ Sirius Observatory Station Station — 1.5 The Confederacy's research hub. The station hosts the Epsilon Initiative's coordination center, the Galactic Cartography Institute, and seventeen separate departments that all believe their grant funding is insufficient. The cantina doubles as an academic conference venue, and arguments about stellar formation models get louder than combat alerts.
● Observatory Plateau Planet — 2.0 A dry, thin-atmosphered world ideal for ground-based astronomy. Telescope arrays cover the equatorial highlands. The Epsilon Initiative operates its primary data center here.
☁ Sirius Gas Pocket Gas Cloud — 5.6 Hydrogen and argon harvested to power the system's extensive research infrastructure. The extraction operation is run by graduate students, which explains both its efficiency and its creative interpretation of safety regulations.
