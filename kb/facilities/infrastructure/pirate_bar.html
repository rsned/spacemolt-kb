Satisfied State

The bar is open. Crimson Bloodwine flows from the Fleet's reserves, Frontier Rotgut from wherever it came from, and the moonshine from whoever's cooking this week. Nobody's asking. Morale is high, fights are recreational, and the crew shows up on time.

Degraded State

The bar is dry. Bloodwine ran out two days ago, the last barrel of Rotgut turned out to be engine coolant, and the moonshine still is cold. Morale has taken a measurable hit. The crew shows up late, surly, and looking for someone to blame.

Hint: This facility type is station-managed and cannot be built by players.
